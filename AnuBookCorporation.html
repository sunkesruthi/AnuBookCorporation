About Us

With a legacy of 63 years in the publishing industry, Sukumar books has been a trusted name in educational resources. Our books are designed to be simple, concise, and easy to understand, making learning accessible to students of all levels. Specializing in self-study materials for primary school students (Classes 1-5), our books serve as a valuable learning aid for children while also providing helpful guidance for parents. Developed in collaboration with highly experienced and skilled teachers, our content aligns with the Andhra Pradesh and Telangana state syllabi and is available in both English and Telugu mediums.

We offer a range of study materials, including Adhunika Mulyankana Darsini (AMD) - an all-in-one study resource covering all subjects for Telugu and English medium students in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Our collection also includes copywriting books in Telugu (5 book series), English (5 book series), Hindi (5 book series), and Cursive writing (5 book series), along with workbook answers for Andhra Pradesh syllabus in both Telugu and English mediums.

At Anu Book Corporation, we are committed to delivering high-quality, affordable educational books that empower students. Our dedication to excellence has made our books a preferred choice among teachers, who frequently recommend them to students and colleagues. With a focus on clarity and effectiveness, we continue to shape young minds through well-crafted, reliable study materials.
